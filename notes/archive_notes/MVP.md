# MVP — One Complete Combat Loop

## In Scope

- 5 Classes 2 Specs Each 
- Basic stats: HP, damage, armor, crit — no mastery, no haste scaling yet
- 1 handcrafted boss with exactly 2 scripted mechanics (e.g., AOE at 50% HP, enrage at 25%)
- Combat tick loop at 500ms — ability priority fires automatically
- Health bars for all 5 heroes + boss during combat
- 1 loot item drops on boss kill (hard-coded, no RNG table yet)
- Win screen (boss dead) / Loss screen (party wiped)
